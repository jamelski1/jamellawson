Poultry Management

REDI and VSU Enactus facilitated two poultry management workshops for Wakonyre Women’s Group, taught by a successful poultry farmer from nearby Kisumu. The workshops covered a multitude of topics and procedures for effective poultry management including:

  • Feeding & Watering
  • Vaccinations
  • Coop management and sanitation
  • Interpreting and addressing various chicken behaviors
  • Strategies for maximizing egg productivity
