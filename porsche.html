Mini‑Glossary (plain language)
  • Open relationship: Sex with others allowed; your relationship stays the primary emotional home.
  • Polyamory: Ability to form romantic and/or sexual connections with multiple people consensually.
  • Metamour: Your partner’s partner (someone you may or may not interact with).
  • Compersion: Warm, happy feeling when your partner has joy with someone else (often seen as the opposite of jealousy).
  • NRE: New Relationship Energy — early‑stage excitement that can distort time and priorities.
  • Parallel vs. Kitchen‑table: Parallel = minimal overlap; Kitchen‑table = everyone can comfortably hang out.
  • DADT: Don’t‑Ask‑Don’t‑Tell (minimal sharing). Not recommended for beginners.
