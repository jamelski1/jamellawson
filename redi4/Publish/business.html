Business Management

In June 2013 REDI facilitated a week long training program for the Wakonyre Women’s Group. Sessions were facilitated in a variety of ways, including computer usage, power-point presentations, and interactive workshop style. The sessions coupled basic business knowledge with practical skills development intended to support the efficiency, productivity and profitability.

Workshop session topics included:

  • Incubator Basics
  • Business Management
  • Technical Writing & Record Keeping
  • Profit & Loss Statement & Break-Even Analysis
  • Challenges of Poultry Farming & Poultry Business Success Stories

The objectives of the training were to:

  • Strengthen participants’ understanding of business operations and mechanisms for accountability
  • Teach the differences between ‘backyard’ chicken rearing versus small-scale poultry production
  • Receive input from participants to be incorporated into the supplemental Training Handbook
  • Ensure participants’ strong comprehension of information covered so that they may share with those not in attendance.
