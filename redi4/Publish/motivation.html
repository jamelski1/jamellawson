Motivation

The rural poor are eager to connect to information and opportunities. However, they are most often limited by a lack of transportation, electrical, and communication infrastructures. More often than not, they have been ignored as unreachable by government and international development initiatives. The reality of most rural poor is limited to subsistence farming by which they are unable to meet the minimum needs of their families: food security, education, and healthcare. REDI strives to always think creatively with these farmers to develop innovative and viable solutions.
