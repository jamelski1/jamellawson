REDI is an organization committed to improving the standard of living for the poor in rural areas. We accomplish this objective by developing well research solutions that empower rural communities to fortify and stabilize the sources of their livelihoods. We envision rural communities that are well equipped with vital information and adequate human capital for designing, implementing, and sustaining cutting-edge development initiatives.

Our main goals are:

  • To equip rural communities with resources that make it possible for them to lead and participate in research for appropriate and effective solutions for problems they face.
  • To collaborate with a wide range of actors and organizations that seek to address issues pertaining to poverty.
  • To share best practices with other rural communities and organizations that seek to improve living standards in rural areas.
  • To systematically and effectively monitor the progress of income generating projects undertaken by the poor in rural areas
