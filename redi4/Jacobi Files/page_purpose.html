Purpose

REDI seeks to assist rural poor farmers in gaining access to information, capital, and support. Our goal is to empower rural smallholder farmers to engage in small business initiatives by assisting them in acquiring startup capital, expert advice, and technical assistance, and providing a long-term support network.

The challenge is in the small things. People cannot foresee everything, nor plan a business to the last bag of chicken feed. That is why REDI is committed to maintaining long term relationships with our clients. We seek to assist our clients to make the most of learning opportunities inherent in the entrepreneurial experience through business analysis, monitoring, feedback, networking with local and international organizations, and financial safety nets
