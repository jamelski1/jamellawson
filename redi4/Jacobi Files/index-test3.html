Rural Empowerment & Development Innovations, Inc. is a non-profit committed empower rural communities to fortify and stabilize the sources of their livelihoods. We envision rural communities that are well equipped with vital information and adequate human capital for designing, implementing, and sustaining cutting-edge development initiatives.

In June 2013, REDI facilitated the initiation of a poultry farming project for a women's community based organization (Wakonyre) in rural south-west Kenya by helping to secure the initial capital and by hosting training workshops on the basics of business management and poultry farming. It continues to support the women's group through consultation regarding current management practices and long-term strategic planning.
