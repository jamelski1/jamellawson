  <p>Where will you likely find yourself the poorest person in town? There are places where even a well-to-do household, by normal standards, would have a hard time keeping up with the Joneses.</p>

<p>To identify the 50 richest areas in the U.S. for Businessweek.com, <a href="http://realestate.yahoo.com/Arkansas/Little_Rock/homes-for-sale;_ylt=AuUNcDE4zYMWLloNJPByxGPxkdEF" target="_blank">Little Rock</a> data company Gadberry Group looked at all Zip Codes in the U.S. with more than 100 households and ranked them by the average income and net worth of their households. The area’s population change since 2000 was also taken into consideration, though only as a minor factor, and no areas with a decrease in number of households, where wealth might become less concentrated, were included. The results include many places around <a href="http://realestate.yahoo.com/New_York/Manhattan/homes-for-sale;_ylt=AtmyU_a.hCz2v2BbSO1csv7xkdEF" target="_blank">New York</a>, <a href="http://realestate.yahoo.com/District_of_Columbia/Washington/homes-for-sale;_ylt=AlZ7UBX39Czdinrcu0GMX7bxkdEF" target="_blank">Washington, D.C.</a>, and <a href="http://realestate.yahoo.com/California/Sunnyvale/homes-for-sale;_ylt=AqX6lGaQ.Yr4T8WR8ErHNF_xkdEF" target="_blank">Silicon Valley</a>.</p> 

<p>As Gadberry’s survey mainly captured data on primary residences, many affluent second-home markets, such as the Hamptons on Long Island and <a href="http://realestate.yahoo.com/Colorado/Aspen/homes-for-sale;_ylt=AssGO5EO2S3oXd2TlzWeNv3xkdEF" target="_blank">Aspen, Colo</a>., did not make the top 50. Also, neighborhoods in such urban areas as <a href="http://realestate.yahoo.com/New_York/Manhattan/homes-for-sale;_ylt=Ar4WIQiKWhdZbOcmzt15uhXxkdEF" target="_blank">Manhattan</a> and <a href="http://realestate.yahoo.com/California/San_Francisco/homes-for-sale;_ylt=AgOGOL3AlD5OXVv.yfhkVkTxkdEF" target="_blank">San Francisco</a> that have pockets of extreme wealth did not make the list because they have a greater diversity of income earners.</p>

The predominant population in this the Bel Air area is elite couples and singles.</p><br />

<p>This neighborhood near the foothills of the Santa Monica Mountains, made famous by the television series Beverly Hills, 90210, is dominated by opulent homes. Zillow.com estimates that in September, 90210's median home value was $2,557,700. The area is adjacent to the sixth-richest Zip Code, 90077. The predominant residents of 90210 are executives and professionals in their 40s and 50s with no children.</p><br />
