Summary:

The objects that makes up my Yatzee program are Score Sheet, Dice, Board,
and possibly Round.  The objects work together to take the user from the
of the game to the end. The Dice Class creates the different dice.  The
Driver Class will then create 6 different dice and roll them to change
the number on the die which will be methods of the Dice Class.  The Board
Class will keep track of the number of rolls for a specific round and after
the user decides to pick their dice or once three rolls comes the user must
give the Score Sheet object it's final decision on what it would like the score
to be and where where he'd like to apply it and  the Score Sheet will keep track
of this information.  The game ends when the Score Sheet is completely filled
and the Driver will know the game is over from the Score Sheet and the game will
be over. I may possibly want to give the roll of keeping the information for an
individual Round to the Round Class, but that was a thought I had at the end
of creating all of the objects so after further thought the Round Class may
be implemented.
